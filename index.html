The only degree program in the country that offers a very rich and immersive work experience-based learning scheme.

Apply Today

The Kalvi Liberal Engineering Program is designed for students from top schools across CBSE, ICSE, and IGCSE boards in India and the Middle East. After we receive your application, you will be taken through a predefined selection process that evaluates your academic performance, cognitive abilities, problem-solving skills, and communication skills to see if you meet our eligibility criteria. Alternatively to this evaluation process, you can choose to take an entrance test as well.
