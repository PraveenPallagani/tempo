About Kalvi

The Kalvi Liberal Engineering Program — Transforming the outstanding into the elite

Our goal at Kalvi is to help students achieve real-world readiness while they pursue their education in engineering. We do this through a holistic multidisciplinary curriculum that helps students build cognitive skills to thrive in tomorrow’s world.
