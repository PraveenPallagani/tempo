About Kalvi

Kalvi evolved from the need for a world-class liberal engineering degree program that nurtures future CTOs, CEOs, and Tech Entrepreneurs by developing their personal and professional cognitive skills and technique.

Technology is changing by the day and to support the various business needs computer science education has become one of the most sought-after streams. Tech companies are also one of the largest recruiters too, but the students passing out are unfit when it comes to being industry-ready.

Our Journey

Since 2008, we have enabled employability skills in over 3 million students at colleges and universities around the world. We are trusted by globally ranked IITs and NITs, as well as startup engineering schools.

Our Vision

To provide world-class content with a very rich learning experience that will help meritorious students to pursue their dream career aspirations and provide world-class teaching and a comprehensive learning environment that will enable deserving students to achieve their professional goals.
